Visit Tenali

This city is renowned for art, culture, drama and hence, it is also called Andhra Paris

Top three famous things for Tenali

Tenali municipal office

The Tenali Municipality is the seat of local government and the Tenali municipal council is the legislative body. It was constituted in 1909 and is classified as a special grade municipality.

Tenali Jelibi sweet

Tenali is famous for one sweet called Jelibi it was so delicious to eat and it was so good in tenali.

Chinaravuru Park

The notable Satyanarayana UDA Lake Park (or Chinaravuru park), named after former municipal chairman Ravi Satyanarayana, is maintained by VGTMUDA (now APCRDA).
